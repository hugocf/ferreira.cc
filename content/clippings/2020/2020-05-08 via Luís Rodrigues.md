> **How to write better**
>
> - Get to the point faster.
> - Write to someone who has no idea what you do.
> - Spell out your acronyms.
> - Tell ’em what you’re gonna tell ‘em.
> - Use active voice, not passive voice.
> - Fewer commas. More periods.
