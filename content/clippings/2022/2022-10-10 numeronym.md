# 2022-10-10 numeronym


> A **numeronym** is a number-based word. Most commonly, a numeronym is a word where a number is used to form an abbreviation (albeit not an acronym or an initialism).

Examples related with software:

> * a11y – accessibility
> * a16z – Andreessen Horowitz[15]

> * c12s – Communications

> * g11n – globalisation / globalization
> * h7k – hyperlink
> * i14y – interoperability

> * i18n – internationalization[14]

> * k8s – Kubernetes[16][17]

> * l10n – localisation[14]

> * m12n – modularisation / modularization
> * o11y – Observability[18]

> * p13n – personalization
